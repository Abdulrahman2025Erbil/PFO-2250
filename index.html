About Resolution 2250

The United Nations Security Council resolution 2250 was unanimously adopted on December 9, 2015, acknowledging the efforts of youth in peacebuilding and providing a set of guiding principles for policies and programs by member states, the United Nations, and civil society.

National Level Impact - Iraq

2016 - Beginning of YPS Advocacy

PFO started working to enhance the role of youth in peacebuilding and decision-making in Iraq

December 10, 2020 - Coalition Launch

National Coalition for Youth, Peace and Security in Iraq

Launched in partnership with UNFPA, FBA, and Ministry of Youth and Sports

A qualitative leap in implementing Resolution 2250 in Iraq

  • Platform for young people to express opinions
  • Constitutional and legal ceiling for protection
  • Government support from Ministry of Youth and Sports
  • Equal representation for men and women

Established to promote and implement the Resolution 2250 agenda in Iraq
